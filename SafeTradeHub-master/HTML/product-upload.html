Welcome to SafeTradeHub. By listing products on our platform, you agree to abide by our rules and regulations designed to ensure a safe and transparent marketplace for everyone.

2. Listing Accuracy

Sellers must provide accurate information about their products, including condition, specifications, and potential flaws. Misleading listings are strictly prohibited.

Illegal items, weapons, hazardous materials, and counterfeit goods are strictly forbidden. Our AI-driven verification system scans all listings for restricted content.

All transactions must go through the SafeTradeHub escrow system. This protects both buyers and sellers by holding funds until the item is received and verified.

In case of any disagreement, SafeTradeHub provides a dedicated dispute resolution service. Staff members will review the case and make a final decision based on evidence provided.
